#1 New Website for Tritones

    *Page screen shots, still undergoing deployment process.

Watch demo video!

In a collaborative endeavor, my teammates and I took on the design and build of the new website for UCSD's premier a cappella group - the Tritones.

Role and Responsibilities

As a frontend developer on the team, my primary responsibilities encompassed:

  • Implementing the design crafted by our design team in Figma using Angular.
  • Engaging in a cooperative effort with the backend team to determine data storage and establish a seamless connection between frontend and backend operations.

Technologies Used:

  • Frontend: Angular
  • Backend: Django

Some Achievements & Features:

  • Integrated a Spotify album list that directly links to the Tritones' albums on Spotify.
  • Developed a scrolling sidebar, booking form, and other interactive features.
  • Enabled backend storage for member profiles and historical photos, allowing easy updates. Any changes made to the backend data are seamlessly reflected on the website, ensuring up-to-date content.
